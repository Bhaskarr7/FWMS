What is FWMS

FWMS is a web application that serves as a dual platform for F&Bs and charities to connect with one another. It aims to minimize food wastage by redistributing excess food from F&Bs to charities.

What is food waste management system?

Food waste is a global problem, with millions of tonnes of food being wasted every year. This not only leads to the loss of valuable resources but also presents significant environmental, economic, and social challenges. The urgency to find effective solutions is amplified by the fact that millions of people around the world suffer from hunger and malnutrition. Therefore, there is a critical need for an efficient food waste management system that can address both the environmental impact and the issue of hunger. This project aims to develop a robust system for managing food waste by facilitating the redistribution of excess food from food and beverage establishments to NGOs and charitable organizations. By establishing effective channels and partnerships, this system will help reduce waste while providing food to those in need.

How can we manage food waste?

Here are few steps which we can follow in order to minimize food wastage:
1) Reduce food waste at home by buying only what you need, planning meals, and storing food properly.
2) Compost food scraps to create nutrient-rich soil for plants.
3) Donate extra food to food banks, shelters, and other organizations.
4) Recycle food packaging and containers.
5) Use leftovers creatively in new meals and recipes.
6) Contact your local government to find out what food waste programs are available in your area.
